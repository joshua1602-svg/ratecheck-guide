How to Challenge

  1. Find your property at gov.uk/find-business-rates
  2. Select 'Check your rateable value' and sign in or create a business rates valuation account
  3. Submit a Check — confirm the facts about your property are correct
  4. If the Check does not resolve it, submit a Challenge using the comparables in this report as evidence
  5. If unsuccessful, you have the right to appeal to the Valuation Tribunal
Important: From April 2026, you have 3 months from the date your new rateable value takes effect to submit a formal Challenge. Do not delay.
